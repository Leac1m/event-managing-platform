# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Event Managing Platform community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Use welcoming and inclusive language
- **Being collaborative** - Work together to solve problems
- **Being constructive** - Provide helpful feedback and criticism
- **Being patient** - Understand that people have different backgrounds and knowledge levels
- **Being honest** - Be truthful in your interactions
- **Being empathetic** - Understand and share the feelings of others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination based on any characteristic
- Offensive, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Sharing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Other conduct that violates laws or platform policies

## Our Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action in response to instances of unacceptable behavior
- Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, commits, code, and other contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct
- Banning users who violate this Code of Conduct

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **For GitHub Issues/PRs**: Report through GitHub's built-in reporting tools
2. **For Sensitive Issues**: Email conduct@eventmanagingplatform.com with:
   - Description of the incident
   - People involved
   - Context and evidence
   - Your contact information

### Investigation & Response

1. All reports will be investigated promptly and fairly
2. Confidentiality of reporters will be maintained
3. Appropriate action will be taken based on findings
4. Decisions will be communicated to relevant parties

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary ban from community spaces
- Permanent ban from community spaces
- Other actions as deemed appropriate

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces (GitHub, discussions, issues, PRs)
- Official project spaces
- Public spaces where someone represents the project or community
- Private communications related to project business
